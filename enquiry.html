What happens next?

A careful enquiry process.

  1. We review your enquiry.
  2. We contact you for a short discovery call.
  3. We check whether companionship-only visits are suitable.
  4. If appropriate, we suggest a companion match.
  5. We arrange a simple first visit.

Service boundary

Companionship only. No personal care, medical care, medication, cooking, cleaning, lifting, mobility assistance, financial handling or emergency support.
